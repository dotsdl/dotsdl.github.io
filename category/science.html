Blogging at BPS 2016!

Thu 25 February 2016 | tags: bloggingbiophysicsconference

The 2016 Biophysical Society Annual Meeting in Los Angeles begins this Saturday, and I’m happy to announce that I’ll be blogging for the conference blog during the course of the week. My focus will be on new developments in software, particularly open source projects.

If you have a project you’d like some exposure for, or just want to talk shop, feel free to ping me on twitter (@dotsdl). See you in LA!

— david
