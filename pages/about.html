about me

I’m David Dotson, a graduate student in the Center for Biological Physics at Arizona State University. I specialize in molecular dynamics simulations of biomolecules, which allow us to probe the mechanisms of protein function to atomic detail. I am a Python enthusiast, Linux nerd, and an advocate for the use of open source software in science.

I currently work in the Beckstein Lab.
